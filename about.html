Who we are

Farmcenta is Nigeria’s fastest growing agri-tech company that provides farmers with essential resources such as farm inputs, smart farming skills and access to premium market. We engage in a sustainable business model across the agricultural value chain for maximum contribution to global food security.

Since 2018, Farmcenta has worked with over 5,000 farmers across Nigeria and we cultivated over 10,000 acres of farmland. Our goal is to progressively scale our impact-oriented model to improve the production capacity of low-income farmers.

Our Vision

To be a unique and impact driven agri-business with sustainability within the food value chain.

Our Mission

To engage in sustainable agricultural production using contemporary technology to support global food security.

Our Mantra

Improving Rural Farming Sustainably
